# Creature Proposal Miriam Daily
## Unicorn
## Health 100
## Attack Description
Does 15 damage each attack. Has a 20% chance to heal itself for 10 health points when it attacks.
## Special Ability (optional)
(Keep it simple — 1–2 sentences)
Can use its horn to block one attack every 5 turns, reducing the damage taken by half for that attack.
## Weakness
(What makes it NOT overpowered?)
Has a moderate attack damage but lacks any strong offensive abilities. The healing ability is random and may not always work
