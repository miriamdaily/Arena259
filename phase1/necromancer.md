# Eldon Pouliot Creature Proposal
## Necromancer
## Health: 100
## Attack: 
The necromancer's attack will be a weak single attack, maybe 5-10 damage. Every 2nd attack will apply a stacking debuff called "target".
## Special ability:
Raise dead - The necromancer can reanimate the dead to help fight on their side. Minion damage is increased by the amount of target stacks an opponent has.
## Weakness:
The minions will have low stats, each one could be taken out in 1-2 hits depending on the opposing creature.
