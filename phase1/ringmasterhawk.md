# ringmasterhawk Ethan Schilder
## Name 
Ringmaster Hawk
## Health 
85
## Attack Description
When the RingMaster Hawk attacks it does 10 damage on each attack, but after 4 successful attacks it'll do a special attack called: final call blow dealing 45 damage.

## Special Ability: Emergency Sideshow!
A ringmaster is nothing without his circus supporting them! If an attack fails or the Ringmaster Hawk takes 30+ damage from one attack the Ringmaster Hawk will call from 7 various birds "acts" who'll try to distract the target if they succeed it'll allow the Ringmaster Hawk to heal or deal some damage (At most 50% of damage dealt last succesful attack or 25% damage dealt healed).
## Weakness: The Circus fold... 
 The circus has to sleep sooner or later the Ringmaster Hawk is no different. After a Curtain call attack the Ringmaster Hawk must take 1 turn to rest during that turn they're guarnteed to be hit by any attack during that turn.
